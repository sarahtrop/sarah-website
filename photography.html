Sports

I am a freelance photographer for the Grinnell College Scarlet & Black newspaper, and although I take photos for a variety of subjects depending on the need of the paper week to week, I focus mostly on sports photography.

While at home, I practice my sports photography as well because both of my siblings as well as many of my friends participate in sports of many types. Baseball and ultimate frisbee are my favorite sports to take pictures of, but I also have experience with basketball, soccer, cross country, track and field, and tennis.

Portraits and Theater

The portrait photography I do is all on my own time, mostly with my friend Carsen. She is also a photographer, so we practice on each other. She and I have spent many afternoons taking portraits of each other around our campus, in varying weather conditions. Our favorite enviornment to take photos in is the snow, which we have quite a lot of in Iowa winters.

I also enjoy taking photos of the various theater productions that happen on campus, as I used to participate in quite a lot of theater when I was younger. Grinnell's mainstage productions are high quality, elaborate performances full of supremely talented individuals, and I consider it a priveledge to get to document their amazing hard work on stage.
