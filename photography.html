Contra Dancing

Contra dancing is a communinty-based folk dance originating in New England. It is loosely based on English Country Dancing, from the same area, but tends to be set to more upbeat music and is more intricate in moves. There are contra dance communities all over the country, and I found contra dancing through my parents, who met dancing in Seattle. I have been dancing my entire life, and started taking photographs of dancing in the last couple of years to document the activity that I love to spend so much of my time doing. Contra dancers are an amazing community of people, and I strive to capture the community and friendships I have found through my camera.
