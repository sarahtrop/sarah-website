Grinnell AppDev

I have been working as a UI Designer and now Webmaster for Grinnell AppDev since December 2014. I have worked to redesign parts of the website, including the past redesign of the Members page. My current project is to redesign the KDIC 88.5 app for iOS and Android.

KDIC 88.5

The KDIC 88.5 app is the mobile application for the Grinnell College radio station. The station is run by students, and the app was developed by AppDev student developers after I designed, prototyped and tested with some help from the current Design Lead.

Splash

This splash is the loading screen for the app, it pops up every time the app is opened, before it loads to the main schedule screen. The background image is a copyright free image from Unsplash.com, and the logo is the official logo of KDIC 88.5.
