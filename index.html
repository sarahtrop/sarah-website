work

Grinnell AppDev

I am currently the Director of Design on the Application Development team, Grinnell AppDev. AppDev is a team of select Grinnell College students who work to design and develop mobile applications usable within the Grinnell community. I have worked on the redesign of current apps, as well as website pages. I was a UI Designer and the webmaster for a semester, and have just been promoted to Design Lead, as well as webmaster.

CS Mentoring

This past spring I was a mentor (for the second time) for the introductory Computer Science Course at Grinnell, CSC 151 - Functional Problem Solving. This class is conducted in the Scheme langauge using DrRacket. I took this class previously as a part of my major and enjoyed it immensely, so when I was asked to be a mentor, I jumped at the opportunity. Mentoring is one of the highlights of my week, and the second semester was just as fun and rewarding as the first. This fall I will be mentoring a new course at my school, entitled "The Art of Code", which I'm super excited about. The class is entirely online, and will be a learning experience for me as much as for the students in the course.
ALSO Italian

I am an Italian tutor in the Alternative Language Study Option at Grinnell, a program that provdes individual driven learning with peer tutors whom are fluent in the chosen language. Last spring, I taught Italian II and restructured the curriculum and classroom activities on our new platform, Haiku Learning. Last spring I was working in an administrative capacity as a Project Manager, as well as helping out the Italian tutors when they need a substitute or learning materials specific to their classes. This fall, I will be continuing my Project Manager duties as well as returning to tutoring Italian II.
