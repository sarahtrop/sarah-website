About Me

I am a second-year computer science major at Grinnell College. On campus, I am currently working for Grinnell AppDev as a UI Designer, as a computer science mentor for CSC151 Functional Problem Solving, as a freelance photographer for the Scarlet & Black newspaper, and as an Italian II tutor in the Alternative Language Study Option program.

In past summers, I have worked for the Pacific Science Center first as a Science Educator-in-Training as part of their teen volunteer program, and then as a Camps Aide for the Camps for Curious Minds. I worked in a similar junior counselor capacity at the Discovery Park Nature Day Camps for multiple summers.

I hope to continue working in mobile app design and web design in the future, and am currently applying to spend Summer 2015 either at the Smithsonian Instutite working in design and production, or at the Seattle Art Museum doing the same types of projects.

If you would like to know more specifically about my experience outside and at Grinnell, the link to my LinkedIn profile is at the bottom of the page.
